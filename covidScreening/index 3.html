COVID-19

Coronavirus disease (COVID-19) is an infectious disease caused by a new virus.

The disease causes respiratory illness (like the flue) with symptoms such as a cough, fever, and in more sever cases, difficulty breathing. You can protect yoruself by washing your hands frequently, avoidign touching your face, and avoiding close contact (1 meter or 3 feet) with other who are unwell.

How it spreads

Coronavirus disease spreads primarily thorough contact with an infected person when they cough or sneeze. It also spreads when a person touches a surface or object that has the virus on it, then touches their eyes, nose, or mouth.
